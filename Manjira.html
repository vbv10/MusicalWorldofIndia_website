Manjira

Description :

The Manjira is a traditional percussion instrument of India. In its simplest form, it is a pair of small hand cymbals. Manjira has a significant importance in Indian folk music. Basically manjira is played in the aarti of Indian Gods & Goddesses for devotional music.They often accompany folk or devotional music. It is used in various religious ceremonies of India, especially bhajans. The manjira is an ancient instrument. They produce a rhythmic tinkling sound when struck together. The sound's pitch varies according to their size, weight and the material of their construction.This handmade cymbal instrument has been crafted by skilled artisans from India.
