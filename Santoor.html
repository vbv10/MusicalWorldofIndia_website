Santoor

The Santoor is the only Indian classical Instrument which is struck.The santoor, an instrument recently adapted in Hindustani classical music, is among the most outstanding developments of this century. The Indian santoor instrument is a trapezoid-shaped hammered dulcimerand played with two wooden sticks.
