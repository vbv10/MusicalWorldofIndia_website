Nagara

Description :

The Nagara is a drum used in the Middle East.Nagaras were treated as war drums. The loud and thundering beat of the huge drums usually meant that the army was marching into the battle.Their structure is like that of a bowlh.The larger is called bam and the smaller one is called zil.Nagara is a conical drum played with two sticks.The Nagara has been called the loudest drums in India.

Audio :

Material : wood, iron, metal, and leather

Drumsticks : length= 25-27cm ; diameter of the drumsticks= 1-1.5 cm

Head Diameter : 22 cm (bam) , 16 cm (zil)
