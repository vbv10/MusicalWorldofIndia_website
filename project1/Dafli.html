Dafli

Description :

A Dafli is a handheld bass producing device, which can be thought of similar to a drum or tabla. There is not much learning involved unlike other musical instruments and one can soon learn to produce a musical pattern on it.Some people earn their livelihood by playing such musical instruments.

Other names : dafli, dap, def, tef, defi, gaval, duf, duff, dof etc.

Audio :

Material : wood or fibre , pairs of small metal jingles

Size : 12 inches

Weight : 200g
